Workout Plans for Every Schedule

Find a plan that fits your busy lifestyle and start today!

10-Minute Morning Stretch

A quick routine to wake up your muscles and improve flexibility.

  1. Neck Rolls - 30 seconds
  2. Shoulder Shrugs - 30 seconds
  3. Cat-Cow Stretch - 1 minute
  4. Hamstring Stretch - 1 minute

Lunch Break Walk

Step away from your desk and recharge with a brisk walk.

  1. Walk at a moderate pace for 10 minutes
  2. Add intervals of jogging if possible
  3. Finish with deep breathing exercises

Evening Strength Routine

Build strength without equipment in just 15 minutes.

  1. Push-Ups - 10 reps
  2. Squats - 15 reps
  3. Plank - 30 seconds
  4. Repeat 3 times
